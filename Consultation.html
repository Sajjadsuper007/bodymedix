Conultation at Clinic

Your special treatment journey to have a dream body will firstly begin with an hour- long consultation with one of our expert at our face –to- face clinics, where we will discuss and evaluate you holistically and design, create and tailor a personalised treatment plan to fit your lifestyle .A personal treatment programme will fully explain to you.

In the face-to-face consultation, the expert gathers information about the individual concerns and evaluate about:

Weight loss goals
Body Shapes
Lifestyle such as diet, exercise, smoking, and alcohol
Medical history including existing illnesses
Family history
Personal steps undertaken to reduce weight and their outcomes
Person has been overweight throughout his life
How the weight gain affects the socially ,psychologically and physically
Medications and remedies taken that may be related to weight gain
Stress factors
Other health concerns
